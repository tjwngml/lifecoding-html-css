Existentialism

Existentialism is the philosophy and literary trend of the 20th century (the general flow of thought of an era). It is a question of "What am I? And what should I do?" and refers to the way of human existence that forms self through choice. Humans have no choice but to choose their own life as long as they are born, and they are overwhelmed by anxiety about these choices and are creating their own future through freedom of choice.

It took place actively in France and Germany in the 1940s and 1950s.
